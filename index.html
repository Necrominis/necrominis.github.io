Necrominis Mini-Painting Studio

I'm Tucker from Necrominis, and I'm a miniature painter hobbyist.

I first tried painting miniatures in 2015, for the Dungeons & Dragons tabletop roleplaying game. In 2019, I took on miniature painting as a serious hobby and have been improving my craft every since.

I still paint miniatures for tabletop games like D&D, Warhammer, and Star Wars: Legion, but now I also paint for the sole purpose of painting beautiful display miniatures, as well.

I've also purchased an AnyCubic Photon S, in 2020, and have been 3D printing3D printing is the construction of physical objects from a digital 3D model file. resin miniatures ever since. Most of the models I print are purchased from My Mini Factory or from artists I support on Patreon.
